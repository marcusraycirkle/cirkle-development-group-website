We are the Cirkle Development Group

Paragraph 1:The Cirkle Development Group (Cirkle Dev Group) is the parent organization established to own, manage, and provide strategic direction for all descendant companies under its corporate umbrella, including the original Cirkle Development. We are structured as a holding group dedicated to maximizing the efficiency and growth of our portfolio businesses. Our existence ensures that each subsidiary can focus entirely on its core mission and operations while benefiting from the financial stability, centralized resources, and clear legal framework provided by the Group. We are building a coherent, synergistic network where stability and strategic oversight drive collective success.

Paragraph 2: Our primary function is to serve as the corporate backbone, handling all critical, high-level administrative responsibilities across the entire Group. This involves comprehensive management of essential financial and legal processes, including payslip handling, ensuring corporate compliance, managing liability issues, and processing all legal documentation. Furthermore, the Group is responsible for standardizing and executing the entire staff recruitment process, ensuring we attract and place top-tier talent where it is needed most. By centralizing these complex functions, the Cirkle Dev Group mitigates risk and ensures standardized, professional operations for every company we own.

Paragraph 3: Ultimately, the Cirkle Development Group is the strategic nexus of our collective ventures. We are responsible for cultivating and approving new alliances and acquisitions, defining the overarching financial and business goals, and representing the interests of the entire family of companies. Our commitment is to clear visibility in all internal and external communications, ensuring that all stakeholders recognize the robust parental structure that supports every entity. Any major corporate updates, press releases, or official announcements regarding our descendant companies and future plans will be issued directly from the Cirkle Development Group itself.

Our Goal

The core mission of the Cirkle Development Group is to serve as the unified engine that powers multiple descendant companies for the most efficient and robust operation possible. We exist to eliminate administrative overlap and resource waste, centralizing critical functions like finance, recruitment, and legal compliance under one expert roof. Our goal isn't just to own groups, but to empower them, allowing each specialized business to dedicate its full energy to its unique objectives, ensuring the entire Group runs as a streamlined, high-performance collective.

Aer Lingus PTFS

Established in 2025, Aer Lingus on PTFS has quickly become a prominent name in the PTFS Community. We've dedicated ourselves to recreating the authentic Aer Lingus experience, from the meticulous detail of our operations the professionalism of our staff. With a rapidly expanding route network covering over 10 destinations, our goal is to provide a premium and immersive flying experience for all virtual aviators. At Aer Lingus PTFS, our commitment to excellence is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that with passion and dedication, we can become one of the most successful and respected airlines on the platform. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every flight, whether a short-haul domestic route or a long-distance international journey, is managed with the utmost care and precision. We are not just a virtual airline; we are a community of aviation enthusiasts striving to achieve the highest standards of realism and service. Our journey is just beginning, and we are excited to expand our fleet, add more exciting destinations, and welcome new pilots to our growing family.
